# Roblox is updating how badge *checking* works.

On March 23, 2026 (only ONE month from now) Roblox will be completely disabling unauthenticated badge checking.

**What does this mean???**
Basically, badge checking (which is how we check your tower completions), is not only going to become a lot slower, but we will also no longer be able to check your stats if your inventory is private. (And no, you currently cannot make your badges public without making your **ENTIRE** inventory public.)

**Well, what next?**
Honestly, I'm not sure yet. Checking **everyone's** progress is going to likely be a thing of the past now, which sucks a lot.
